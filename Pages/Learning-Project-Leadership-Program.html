Learning Project Leadership Program

Time:

30hrs

Delivery Method:

Zoom/Teams

Schedule:

10 weeks (6hrs every 2 weeks or 3hrs every week)

In this program you will determine learning project topics based on leadership opportunities/issues within your company. This program is suitable for 12-30 learners from all verticals of your company at the management level. Unique prework will be assigned based upon your learning project topics as well as work to do during the weeks between virtual classrooms.

Getting to know the Group

Content Timing High Level Design
Introduction 1.5 hrs Leadership Welcome, Program Outline, and Introduction
Form program teams and introduce learning project topics (as decided by leaders)
Assessment-Debrief 1.5 hrs How We Work Together Workshop based on DISC
Psychometric Assessment (DISC) - personal feedback/coaching debriefs have been conducted prior to the start of the program.
Team Building 2.5 hrs Set up (.5hr)
Experiential Activity - "Virtual Escape Room" (1hr)
Debrief Activity (1hr)
Wrap Up .5 hrs Next Steps
Ensure teams know what their project focus is for the purpose of this program.

Stakeholder Café 2 hrs Group carousel learning activity cont'd
