Anil is the Managing Director of Save it like Sully, an executive presentation training and coaching company. Anil has worked with many individual leaders and groups from various industries including technology, retail, financial services, healthcare, education, industry associations and government. Prior to Save it like Sully Anil held senior positions in research & development, product marketing, communications, and consulting with Hill & Knowlton Strategies, March Networks, and Cognos Inc. (now IBM). Anil works with clients in group and one-on-one settings to help elevate their presentation skills and their executive presence. He is a regular guest lecturer at the University of Ottawa, Queen’s University, University of Toronto and Carleton University.
