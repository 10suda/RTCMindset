Meet Our Coaches

Linda Rummell
Principal
Master Certified Coach (ICF)
"You know best for yourself and it is my job to discover, clarify, and align you with what you want to accomplish."
+ Learn More

Linda is a dynamic, professional, and passionate Leader/Coach with highly developed skills in: Coaching, Leadership and Development, Talent Management, Training and Development, Strategic Thinking (OD/OE/Change Management), and cultural engagement. With a coaching philosophy and practice driven by choice, curiosity, and courage she has successfully coached C-suite executives, leaders at all levels, and students within media, QSR, banking, and logistics companies.

Lorraine Evans
Associate Certified Coach (ICF)
"Quote from Lorraine."
+ Learn More

Lorraine is an executive Human Resources Professional, currently employed by Canada Post as Director of Human Resources, and coaches both within Canada Post and with external clientele. Lorraine's coaching practice includes Executive and Management leadership Coaching, Life Skills, and Business Skills. She has a passion for inclusion, acceptance, and disability management. Lorraine sits on the Board of Directors for the Canadian Society of Disability Management, the Board of Governors for the National Institute of Disability Management and Research, and the Board of Governors for the Pacific Coast University of Workplace Sciences.
